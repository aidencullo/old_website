Websites

https://www.spanishdict.com/ https://context.reverso.net/traduccion/ these two websites are great, spanishdict gives you 100% the correct translation even with breakdowns by spanish country and works offline, context-reverse is great too cause it just picks passages to translate in place words and shows you the entire sentence in spanish.

Consejos (Advice)

Series Narcos (Mexico y Colombia) El Chapo Harina Casa de papel Consejos (advice) 1. Try to learn in a fun/entertaining way otherwise it will be solely frustration and you will be discouraged 2. When it comes time to speak/write just do that to the best of your ability, then later on maybe look up words, don’t try to speak perfectly 3. Focus on listening and speaking, more important than reading/writing (as you would basically be doing those things effectively anyway)
